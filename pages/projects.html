Game of NIM

 

In this game a player takes turns with the computer choosing a number of stones between 1 and 3 to remove from the central pool. The game ends when the last stone is taken, and the player who took the last stone is declared the winner. I added functionality that renders the text with audio in a manner reminiscent of Animal Crossing's Animalese.

Requires ffmpeg and pydub to run.
